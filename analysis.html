3 — What You're Doing Well

Strengths to Build On

  • Ad relevance is excellent. 87% of keywords are rated Above Average — the copywriting and keyword-to-ad alignment is strong.
  • You already have PPC-specific pages. The /ppc/wholesale/, /ppc/distributor/, and /ppc/manufacturing/ paths show awareness that paid traffic needs different treatment. /ppc/wholesale/ even has an embedded form.
  • Product positioning is clear and differentiated. "Unified Commerce + OMS on one data model" is genuinely unique in the market. The messaging is right — the delivery vehicle needs work.
  • Trust signals exist. Forrester Wave Leader, Gartner recognition, strong customer stories (Ace Hardware, Fortis Life Sciences, Wolters Kluwer) — these just need to be more prominent on PPC pages.
  • Content depth is an asset for organic. The 3,500-4,200 word pages are valuable for SEO. The issue is using them for paid traffic where visitors need immediate relevance.
4 — What Competitors Do

How the Market Handles PPC Landing Pages

BigCommerceDedicated /dm/ demo pages with stripped navigation. Forms link to separate pages. Lightweight builds for PPC traffic.
Sana CommerceEmbedded Marketo forms with quantified social proof ("80% sales adoption", "30% revenue growth"). Full site nav still present.
commercetoolsUses product pages with full navigation. No embedded forms. Relies on 3,000+ multi-channel ads and ABM targeting via 6sense.
Salesforce Commerce CloudDedicated form pages at /form/ paths. Very heavy enterprise CMS infrastructure. Forms live on separate pages from product content.
The pattern: Best-in-class competitors use dedicated PPC landing pages with stripped navigation and embedded forms. None of them have an ROI calculator, comparison table, or FAQ schema on their PPC pages — these are all net-new differentiators in our recommendation.
5 — Current vs. Recommended

What Changed

× Current Subfolder Pages

  • WordPress + Elementor (500KB–1.5MB page weight)
  • No embedded lead form on 5 of 6 pages
  • Full site navigation (15+ exit paths)
  • 3,500–4,200 words per page
  • Generic "Watch a Demo" CTAs on every page
  • Homepage used for 4 NB keywords
  • No ROI calculator or comparison content
  • No FAQ schema markup

✓ Recommended PPC Landing Page

  • Static HTML/CSS (~109KB total — 5–10x lighter)
  • Two embedded forms (hero + final CTA)
  • Minimal navigation (logo, phone, CTA only)
  • ~1,800 focused, keyword-dense words
  • Intent-matched CTAs with benefit context
  • Purpose-built for B2B Commerce keyword cluster
  • Interactive ROI calculator + competitive comparison table
  • 6 FAQs with JSON-LD schema for rich snippets
