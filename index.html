Why B2B Teams Switch to KIBO

The Legacy Tech Debt Problem: A Broken B2B Stack

Most B2B companies run a patchwork of 3-5 platforms for commerce, OMS, inventory, and portals. The result: data silos, delayed orders, and frustrated buyers.

Without KIBO The Patchwork Problem

  • Separate systems for commerce, OMS, and inventory with batch syncing
  • Price discrepancies between the portal and ERP
  • Inventory counts stale by hours — oversells and split shipments
  • Buyers call in for order status because the portal can't show it
  • 6-12 month implementations to add one new channel
  • High-volume tasks require constant human “glue.”

With KIBO One Unified Platform

  • Commerce + OMS + Inventory on a single data model — real-time sync
  • Contract pricing applied consistently across every channel
  • Real-time ATP/CTP inventory across DCs, stores, and vendors
  • Full self-service: order status, reorders, invoices, returns
  • Modular deployment — go live in 8-12 weeks, expand incrementally
  • AI Agents to autonomously route orders, resolve exceptions, and handle replenishment with guardrails
